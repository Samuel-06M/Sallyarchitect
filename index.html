About Me

Hello! I am an architect with a passion for creating functional and aesthetic spaces that blend seamlessly with their surroundings. With a background in both design and project management, I bring a unique perspective to every project I undertake. I graduated from Chuka University, where I honed my skills in architectural design and sustainable building practices.
My career has been dedicated to exploring innovative solutions and pushing the boundaries of architecture. Whether it's residential, commercial, or public projects, I am committed to delivering designs that not only meet but exceed client expectations.

My Services

Architectural Design

Transform your vision into reality with bespoke architectural designs that reflect your unique style and needs. I offer comprehensive design services, from initial concept sketches to detailed plans and 3D visualizations, ensuring a seamless and engaging design process.

Streamline your construction projects with expert project management. I oversee every phase of the project, from planning and budgeting to execution and completion, ensuring that projects are delivered on time, within budget, and to the highest standards.
